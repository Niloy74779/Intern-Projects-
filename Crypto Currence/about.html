We Are BitCoin

Bitcoin is based on a protocol known as the blockchain, which allows to create, transfer and verify ultra-secure financial data without interference of third parties.

Bitcoin is a cryptocurrency, a form of electronic cash. It is a decentralized digital currency without a central bank or single administrator that can be sent from user to user on the peer-to-peer bitcoin blockchain network without the need for intermediaries. It is powered through blockchain technology.
